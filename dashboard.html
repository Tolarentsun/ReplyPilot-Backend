Manual Only
📋 How to import Yelp reviews
1. Go to your business page on yelp.com
2. Copy a customer review
3. Click "+ Add Review" above and select Yelp as the platform
4. Paste the review — ReplyPilot will analyze it and generate an AI response
5. Copy the AI response and paste it back into Yelp manually
Open Yelp ↗
⚠️ Yelp does not allow automated review imports or response posting. Even paid Yelp API plans only provide 7 reviews max. Manual copy-paste is the most reliable method — connect Google for full automation.
