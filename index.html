A note on automatic review collection

We are actively working on building a seamless interface that automatically collects your reviews the moment they are posted — no copy-pasting required. Google Business Profile currently allows us to sync reviews and post responses directly on your behalf. However, Yelp, Facebook, and TripAdvisor impose strict API restrictions that limit or prevent automated access to review data. Until those platforms open up broader access, reviews from those sources can be added manually in seconds using our "Add Review" tool — ReplyPilot will still analyze them and generate a response you can copy and paste back. We will update this as platform access improves.

How It Works
